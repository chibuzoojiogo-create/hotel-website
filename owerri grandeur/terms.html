2 Cancellation & Modification Policy

We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both guests and the hotel:

  • Standard Rate: Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Cancellations within 48 hours will incur a charge of one night's room rate
  • Non-Refundable Rate: No refund will be issued for any cancellation or modification after booking
  • Group Bookings (5+ rooms): Special cancellation terms apply — please refer to your group contract
  • Modifications are subject to availability and any applicable rate differences

To cancel or modify a reservation, please contact our reservations team at least 48 hours before your scheduled arrival.

3 Check-in & Check-out

  • Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM. Early check-in is subject to availability and may incur an additional charge
  • Standard check-out time is 12:00 PM (noon). Late check-out may be arranged at an additional fee, subject to availability
  • A valid government-issued photo ID (National ID, International Passport or Driver's Licence) is required at check-in for all guests
  • Guests must be at least 18 years of age to check in without a parent or guardian

4 Guest Conduct

Owerri Grandeur is committed to providing a peaceful and luxurious environment for all guests. We ask that all guests respect the following:

  • Quiet hours are observed between 11:00 PM and 7:00 AM in all guest room areas

  • Pets are not permitted on the premises unless specifically agreed in writing prior to arrival
  • Any damage caused to hotel property by a guest will be charged to that guest's account
  • The hotel reserves the right to remove any guest whose conduct is deemed disruptive, offensive or contrary to the safety of other guests or staff
